Tutors

Teach students in need and get volunteer hours.

Registration for the Spring 2023 semester is open! Apply now with the form below.

Program Details

Session Details
Tutor one hour classes, one time a week, via Google Meets. Our spring program runs for 17 weeks, from January 3rd to May 22nd. Class resources and lession plans are provided. You may teach more than one class.



Aim
Help with homework and catch them up with their class, or teach them ahead of what they're learning. Ideally connect with tutees and adapt to their learning to maximize learning and engagement.



Expections
You will need to attend a training session if you're a new tutor. Student absences are required to be reported for class attendance. You will also be largely responsible for rescheduling classes and communicating to the parents.

Your Impact

By volunteering at Virtual Tutoring SF, not only do you provide students the help they need for success in class, you also provide families with assurance and take the worry off of them knowing that their child is going to succeed. Hear what parents have to say about tutors like you!
